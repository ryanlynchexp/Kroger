Executive Summary

The ask, and our answer

The Ask

Materially reduce IT spend in 2026 — without disruption.

Kroger asked Experis to find significant, near-term cost savings across IT resourcing and services, while protecting delivery, active projects, and the contract workforce.

  • Lower the cost of the current run rate
  • Protect delivery continuity & talent
  • Move quickly, with minimal program risk
Our Answer

A four-lever value realization plan.

Stacked savings — bill rate, holiday furlough, AI & automation, and vendor streamlining — executed with our proven transition framework to keep delivery steady.

Up to ~$11M+
  • 10% bill rate adjustment + furlough
  • $1M target from AI & automation
  • $1–7M from vendor streamlining
  • Modernized rate card for steady-state savings
